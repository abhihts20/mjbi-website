About

MJ Biotech Industries

M.J Biotech Industries, one of the evolving agriculture products manufacturing firm in India. We are one of the leading producers and marketers of plant fertilizers and nutrients. Our partnership with the farming community is vital. Serving as an imperative link between farmers and consumers, we strive to improve the yield and productivity of the crops and hence help the farmer get a better price for their produce. We facilitate this, through collaboration and innovation and are committed to sharing our global knowledge and experience to meet economic, environmental, and social challenges. Established in 2008, we are headquartered in Hathras. Our extensive distribution facilities span across various states of India.
